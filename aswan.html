Welcome to Aswan

Aswan is another fascinating city in Egypt with a rich history and many important places to visit

Philae Temple

Dedicated to the goddess Isis, this beautiful temple complex is located on Agilkia Island and is famous for its stunning architecture and tranquil surroundings.

Aswan High Dam

An impressive engineering feat, the dam controls the Nile River's flooding and has created Lake Nasser, one of the largest artificial lakes in the world.
